WritePipe
description🌐

WritePipe class abstract🌐 interface🌐

The "write" end of an Pipe created by Pipe.create.

final pipe = await Pipe.create();
pipe.write.add("Hello World!".codeUnits);
await pipe.write.flush();
await pipe.write.close();
